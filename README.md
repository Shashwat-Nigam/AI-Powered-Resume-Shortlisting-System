üéØ Goal
To build a web-based system that automatically reads, analyzes, and ranks resumes according to a company's
specific job requirements.
The system extracts details from resumes, cover letters, and LinkedIn profiles, compares them against a job
description, and produces a ranked shortlist of the best-fit candidates.
It helps recruiters save time, increase accuracy, and make data-driven hiring decisions.

üåç Real-World Use Case
Recruiters and HR teams often receive hundreds of resumes for a single job opening. Manually reading and
shortlisting them is time-consuming, inconsistent, and biased.
This system solves that by automatically:
- Reading resumes, cover letters, or LinkedIn profiles
- Matching key skills, experience, and education to the job requirement
- Scoring each candidate
- Displaying the results on a recruiter dashboard
